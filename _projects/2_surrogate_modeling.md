Developed high-fidelity surrogate models using deep learning techniques to replace expensive numerical simulations. These models provide real-time predictions for complex PDE systems while maintaining accuracy comparable to traditional solvers.

Applications include:
- Fluid dynamics simulations
- Heat transfer modeling
- Structural mechanics optimization
